Every tool,
explained.

Six tools in one app. Here's what each one does.

Radar
A world map of real fossil discoveries.

Your real location, 1.8 million occurrence records, and filters for 74,000+ genera. See which prehistoric species have been found near you — or explore any country on Earth.

Learn more →
FossilRun
The dig game. Every find is real.

An always-on detector warms as you close in on a buried site. A timing-ring mechanic lets you excavate. Every species that surfaces is a real, documented genus with photographs.

Learn more →
Fossil-DEX
74,000+ real prehistoric genera. Sourced.

A searchable catalogue with field dossiers: real photo, scientific classification from the Open Tree of Life, geological age, formation, diet, rarity, and occurrence count.

Learn more →
Continental Drift
540 million years of tectonic history.

Scrub from today back to the Cambrian on a live paleogeographic map. Watch the continents move and see the creatures that lived in each era, powered by GPlates reconstructions.

Learn more →
SIGNAL
Paleontology news. Bilingual, continuous.

A live RSS feed from Phys.org, ScienceDaily, and Sci.News — new species descriptions, dig site discoveries, and research findings in English and Spanish, as they publish.

Learn more →
PALEODEX
Collection & Rank
Your museum. Your rank. Your upgrades.

Every excavated specimen lives in your Museum. An 8-tier Collection Rank reflects what you've collected. Fragments fund five field-kit upgrades. The leaderboard is anonymous and global.

Learn more →
